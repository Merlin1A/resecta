## 4. Your responsibilities and prohibited uses

You are responsible for how you use the App and for verifying redaction results
before sharing any document.

**How redaction works.** Resecta offers two redaction modes that share the same
pixel-destruction process for the regions you mark. Secure Rasterization, the
default, flattens each affected page to an image, which is designed to remove
the underlying text layer for that page. Searchable Redaction is designed to
drop the text you mark while keeping a selectable text layer for the content you
did not redact; for pages where the text cannot be extracted reliably it falls
back to image-only output. In either mode, the App is designed to remove the
original text or image data from the regions you select.

**What the App does not represent.** The App assists with redaction; it does not
promise any particular outcome, makes no representation that any given redaction
is complete, and makes no representation regarding whether redacted content can
later be retrieved or reconstructed. Automated detection can miss sensitive
content, and detection surfaces suggestions for your review rather than acting on
its own. You are responsible for reviewing every page and confirming that
sensitive text, images, and metadata have been redacted before you share a
document. No tool can guarantee perfection. Always verify. <!-- LegalPhrases:safe -->
